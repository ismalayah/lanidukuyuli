Who Was Michael B Jordan Ex Girlfriend Before Lori Harvey? A Look Into The Personal Life Of An Ameri

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, September 17, 2024

Fans want to know who Michael B. Jordan dated before Lori Harvey, who is the adopted daughter of American TV host Steve Harvey.

Jordan is an American actor and film producer who was born on February 9, 1987, to Donna Jordan and Michael A. Jordan in Santa Ana, California. He is 35 years old.

Michael is well-known for his roles as Erik Killmonger in Black Panther, Oscar Grant, a shooting victim in Fruitvale Station, Adonis Johnson in Creed, and Johnny Storm in Fantastic Four.

Who Was Michael B. Jordan’s Ex-Girlfriend Before Lori Harvey?

Before dating the American model, entrepreneur, and socialite Lori Harvey, Jordan was rumored to be dating the Swedish singer Snoh Aalegra. In the music video Whoa by Aalegra, which came out on December 13, 2019, the actor and the singer shared a passionate kiss.

Michael also said that he was a huge fan of the singer, which made people wonder if there was something romantic between them. But they never said if they were dating or had a romantic relationship, so we don’t know if they were or not.

The Dating History And Relationship Timeline Of Michael B. Jordan Explored

Michael is a handsome Hollywood heartthrob whose name has been linked to many famous people, such as Kendall Jenner, the highest-paid model and star of the reality show Keeping Up With the Kardashians.

The Black Panther actor was dating Catherine Paiz in 2014, according to US magazine. They were seen going to the beach and getting close on a Jet Ski in Miami. Later in 2015, it was said that he was dating Kendall because they were seen together leaving an afterparty for the Met Gala.

Later in 2019, Jordan posted a video to Instagram of him and Lupita Nyong’o making out at an afterparty for the Golden Globes. He also said that they were good friends. Before making his relationship with Lori Harvey official, his name was linked to Cindy Bruna, Kiki Layne, and Snoh Aalegra in the same year.

The Reason Behind Michael B. Jordan And Lori Harvey’s Breakup

Yes, Michael is single again because he and his girlfriend Lori Harvey broke up. The cute couple met through friends they both had. They started dating in November 2020 and made it official on Instagram in January 2021.

In 2020 and 2021, the actor also met the girl’s parents and spent Christmas with them. Steve also said that the actor from Black Panther is a good gift-giver, and the TV host was happy for them.

Jordan and Harvey were together for one and a half years, but they broke up in June 2022. According to People, the American model wasn’t ready to settle down, while the film producer was ready for a long-term relationship. Still, it looks like they’re moving on because they’ve both taken down pictures of the couple from their Instagram feeds and stopped following each other.

Are Jeremy Allen White and Rosala dating?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, July 25, 2024

Actor, new Calvin Klein spokesperson, and all-around man-of-the-moment Jeremy Allen White heated up the kitchen when he was spotted out and about with Spanish singer-songwriter Rosalía late last year.

Speculation regarding White and Rosalía began in late October 2023, when Deuxmoi’s anonymous celebrity gossip podcast mentioned sightings of the pair on a potential dinner date in Los Angeles. But is there any truth to the rumor of the two rising stars courting?

Are Jeremy Allen White and Rosalía dating?

As reported by TMZ later that same month, The Golden Globe-winning star of The Bear enjoyed a day trip at the farmers’ market with the Grammy award winner, whose third studio album, Motomami, was one of the most critically acclaimed albums of 2022.

In the candid photos, White appeared to be holding a large bouquet, seemingly presenting a flower to the singer-songwriter, whose real name is Rosalia Vila Tobella. Nothing says traditional courtship like flowers, resulting in many fans interpreting the paparazzi pics as a date.

Previously, White was thought to be dating model Ashley Moore, after they were photographed kissing in the street last summer. The 32-year-old was also briefly linked to actress and singer Selena Gomez ar few weeks prior.

Also in mid-2023, Rosalía and fellow singer and former fiancé Rauw Alejandro announced they had broken up after three years together. In May, White’s now ex-wife, fellow actor Addison Timlin, filed a petition for divorce from White. The former couple, who share two children together, met on the set of Ezra Miller-led psychological thriller Afterschool (2008).

Since their first spotted outing, 31-year-old Rosalía and White have been photographed together pretty sporadically over the remainder of the year. Neither the former Shameless actor nor the BIZCOCHITO singer have made any kind of public statement on their relationship status. However, that hasn’t stopped others from commenting on their behalf.

After being papped together again in November 2023, an accompanying post by Us Weekly confirmed that Jeremy and Rosalía were in fact dating. “Rosalía and Jeremy Allen White have recently started dating,” an anonymous source told the publication. “They started out as just friends, but things have turned romantic recently. The following month, the two were seen out together once more and holding hands, seemingly confirming the source’s claims.

The new celebrity couple appear to have brought their romance into the new year, as photos published on January 4th show the two wearing matching black outfits while out for a stroll in New York City. As for how serious the highly-publicized couple are in their relationship, that is something kept private for now.

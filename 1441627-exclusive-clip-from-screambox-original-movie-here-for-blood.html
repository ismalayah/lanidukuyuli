Exclusive Clip From Screambox Original Movie Here For Blood

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, May 23, 2024

WrestleZone proudly presents an exclusive clip from the Screambox Original film, Here For Blood.

The wrestling-fueled horror-comedy hits select theaters, digital, and Screambox on February 9.

“Struggling to make ends meet, a rowdy pro wrestler agrees to babysit a precocious 10-year-old girl. What starts of as a laid-back night of junk food and video games quickly spirals into bloody chaos as the secluded home is invaded by a cult of mask-wearing maniacs,” reads the official synopsis for the movie.

The Resident Evil franchise’s Shawn Roberts stars as the besieged babysitter, while Twisted Sister frontman Dee Snider lends his legendary voice to a decapitated skull. Maya Misaljevic (The Boys), Joelle Farrow, Tara Spencer-Nairn, and Michael Therriault (Chucky) co-star.”

Professional wrestlers Channing Decker and Marc Andre Boulanger also appear in the film. Roberts also trained with Tyson Dux (WWE, TNA, Ring of Honor) to prepare for the lead role.

Snorlax (Japanese: カビゴン Kabigon) is a Colorless-type Basic Pokémon card. It is part of the Twilight Masquerade expansion.

Card text

But First, Food
はらごしらえ
Attach an Energy card from your hand to this Pokémon. If you do, heal 60 damage from this Pokémon.

Spoilers On Matches For AEW Dark Universal Studios Tapings

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, May 19, 2024

AEW returned to Universal Studios for another round of Dark tapings today. The crowd was packed to see AEW stars and they had quite a few surprises in store for them.

Bryan Danielson made his AEW Dark debut in Universal Studios during these tapings. Also, Tony Nese wrestled for AEW for the first time during today’s event. Cody Rhodes also put on mask and teamed up with Fuego Del Sol for the first time.

The order might be different once they air the episodes, because Tony Khan can stack the shows however he wishes. Khan is obviously accumulating a lot of content for AEW’s library with tapings like this.

You can find the lineup for upcoming AEW Dark matches below. The results are not getting out as they have in the past, but we are able to secure the matches.
